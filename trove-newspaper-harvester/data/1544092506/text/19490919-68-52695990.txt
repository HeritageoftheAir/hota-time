 WORLD TOUR IN EIGHT DAYS LONDONT. - Wynford Vaughan SThomas, well-known B.B.C. commentator, accompanied by Charles de Jaeger, B.B.C. tele vision cameraman, will leave London this morning on a round the-world flight. They plan to do the journey in eight days. Vaughan Thomas and de Jaeger are due in Sydney on Friday, and will leave the next day.
