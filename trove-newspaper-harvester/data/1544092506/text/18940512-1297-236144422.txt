 THE MAIL STEAMERS. A cable was received in Sydney yesterday announcing the arrival of the Canadian- Australian liner Warrimoo at Vancouver, from Sydney, on Thursday, two days in advance of time. She starts on her return trip to the colonics on the 16th inst. The R.M.S. Oruba (Orient line), fiom London en route to Sydney, called at Albany yester day. The Sydney portion of her mails may be expected on Wednesday next.
