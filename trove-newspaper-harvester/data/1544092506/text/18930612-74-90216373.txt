 THE SYDNEY CANADIAN SERVICE. The steamship Miowera, the pioneer boat of Messrs Huddart-Parker's. new service from Sydney to Vancouver, has arrived at the latter place. Her arrival was the occasion for an ovation, and addresses of congratulation were presented to the captain from several Canadian Chambers of Commerce. SYDNEY, SUNDAY. Intimation has been received of the arrival on Friday of the a. Miowera at Vancouver, after a run of 21 days from Sydney and 19 from Brisbane, including one day's stoppage at Honolulu. The Australian mails are expected to reach London on the 30th day out.
