 THE PORT CAROLINE Messrs. Gibbs, Bright and Co are advised that the Anglo- Australian steamer Port Caroline is on route to Sydney, having left London on tho 24th ultimo. The Port Caroline last voyage homeward made the run from Melbourne to Flushing under42 days. She left Albany 2 days l8 hours &nbsp; behind u mail steamer, and arrived at Flushing the same day as the steamer arrived at London.
