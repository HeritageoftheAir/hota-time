 Mail Steamers. The E.M.S. Mooltan leaves Sydney to morrow for London via ports. The E.M.S. Orontes arrived at Sydney yesterday afternoon from Brisbane, and will resume her voyage to London next Wednes day. The E.M.S. Ophir, en route from London to Sydney, called at Fremantle yesterday. Her mails, dated London September 27, will be delivered here next Wednesday. The E.M.S. China arrived at Sydney early yesterday morning from London via ports.
