 RECORD FLIGHT TO LONDON LONDON. April 7 (A.A-P.): A B.O.A.C. Constellation ' reached London tonight almost on time despite leaving Sydney 24 hours late, says Reuters. The pilot cut short overnight stops to catch up the lost day. The total journey took three days four hours 15 minutes, which is a record for any regu lar service on the Australia- London route. No service has succeeded in making up so much lost time in either direction.
