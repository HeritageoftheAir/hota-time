 Imperial Airways Summer Services Accelerated LONDON, Tuesday. Imperial Airways have accelerated the summer services, -ind the new schedule will operate from next Mon day. 'Planes will leave for Australia on Wednesdays, .Saturdays and Sundays restoring the two day schedule to Basra, and three days to Calcutta. Sydney will be reached in nine days two and a quarter hours. Night stops on the homeward run will be Calcutta, Karachi and Basra on a schedule of nine days 14 and three-quarter hours.
