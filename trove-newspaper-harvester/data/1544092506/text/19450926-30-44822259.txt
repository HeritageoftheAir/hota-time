 NEW SEA RECORDS. MAURETANIA'S TRAVELS. 28.600 Sea Miles in 81 Days. LONDON. 'Sept 25.--The liner Mauretania berthed at Liverpool last night after completing a journey of 28,662 sea miles in 81 days 16 hours -a world record. She made the trip from Sydney to the Mersey, a distance of 13,700 miles, in a month and a day, which is also a record.
