 FRIDAY IS POPPY DAY IN SYDNEY TIMING IN WITH KING'S WREATH IN LONDON On Friday Poppy Day will be held in Sydney. All proceeds go to dis tressed Diggers and their dependents. The Governor and Lady Game will attend the Festival of Remembrance, at the Town Hall; also the Premier, Mr. B. S. Stevens, and Mrs. Stevens; the Lord Mayor (Aid. S. Walder) and Mrs. Walder; Brigadier-General Heri tage, State Commandant; and Cap tain, H. J. Feakes, representing the Navy. At 9 p.m., the exact time when the King Is placing his wreath on the Cenotaph In London, the Governor will give a short address.
