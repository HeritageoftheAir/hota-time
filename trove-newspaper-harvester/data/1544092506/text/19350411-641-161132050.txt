 C. &D. LINE. Port Darwin From New York. The Commonwealth and Dominion Line have received a radio message from the master of the. steamer Port Darwin, now en route from New York to Australian ports, stating that he will arrive at Brisbane on the morn ing of the 16th inst., and Sydney four days later. . The Port Fremantle, from London, arrived at Melbourne yesterday morn ing, and is due at Sydney on Satur day.
