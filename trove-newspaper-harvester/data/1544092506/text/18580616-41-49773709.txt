 'MELBOUENE SHIPPING. 'POST PHILLIP HEADS. 'Arrived, this day— Annette, barque, 341 tons, from London, for Geelong, 112 days* passage ; Wanderer, brig, from Sydney ; Don Pedro, brig, from Newcastle; City of Sydney, steamer, from Sydney ; Black Swan, steamer, from Launceston. 'Sailed — Chamois, brig, for Guam; Valiant, brig, for Newcastle; Jannet, brig, for Newcastle ; Woden, ship, for Batavia.
