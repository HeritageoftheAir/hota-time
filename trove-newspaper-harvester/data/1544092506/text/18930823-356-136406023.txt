 THE GENERiL GORDON. THE magnificent four-masted ship General Gordon, which reached Sydney from London in ballast on MIonday, had a splendid passage, and mado splendid thine. She left Beaehy Head on the 20th May, and crossed the line on the 22nd June, 32 days out. From the Crozet Islands to Flidors' Island the vessel averaged 291 per day. the largest day's run being 345 miles,' and the greatest distance covered within a week being 2121 miles. The voyage to Sydney occupied 03 days. The vessel loads wool for the London-January sales.
