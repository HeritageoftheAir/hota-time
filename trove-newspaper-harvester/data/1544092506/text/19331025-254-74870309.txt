 Aviation MR. GREEN'S FLIGHT Karachi, October 17. The Spartan air cruiser Faithful City, piloted by- Captain Lynch Blosse, which is travelling from- London to Sydney and back, arrived here at mid day,- on- the 16th, after a five and a half days' trip from' London. The plane has been chartered by Mr. W- P.' C. Greene. M.P., who is accompanied by Lord Apsley. The travellers yes terday saw the first grass for fe,700 I wiles. They will now tour India for three days and are due at Sydney at the end'of the month.
