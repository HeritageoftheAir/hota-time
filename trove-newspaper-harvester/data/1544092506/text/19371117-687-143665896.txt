 TEN DAYS IN SYDNEY LONDON, Tuesday. According to 'The Times' aviation correspondent, the flying boats which axe coming to Australia will call att Marseilles and other places, arriving in Sydney the last week in January. They will stay ten days and after wards fly to Melbourne, Hobart, Ade laide, Perth, Cjarnarvoh. and 'Derby, whence they leave for home about the middle of March, being due back a/t Plymouth In Miay.
