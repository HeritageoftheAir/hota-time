 Rapid Rise in Tin. SYDNEY, June 1. A further increase in the price of tin occurred at the London Metal Ex- change yesterday, the spot quotation, at £210/7/6 per ton, showing a rise of £10/18/9, and for forward delivery of £11/1/3 per ton, on the previous day's rates. An increase of £19/8/9 and £19/12/6 per ton respectively since Fri- day last.
