 One Day Trip to London London, Wednesday: Bri- tish Overseas Airways Cor- poration, in partnership with Qantas Empire Airways, will begin a regular jet-liner ser- vice from London to Sydney next year with Mark II. Comets. The B.O.A.C. chairman (Sir Miles Thomas) said this tonight. The flying time of two and a half days for the 11,525 miles trip would be cut to slightly more than 24 hours. At present B.O.A.C. and Qantas operate the London Sydney service with Constel- lations.
