 Passenger Plane's Record Flight LONDON, Sun. (A.A.P.).-A Brit- ish Overseas Airways Corporation Constellation plane reached Lon- don almost on time, although it left Sydney 24 hours late. The pilot cut short the overnight stops to catch up the .lost day, and the total journey took three days, four hours, 55 minutes, which is a record for any regular service on the Australia-London route.
