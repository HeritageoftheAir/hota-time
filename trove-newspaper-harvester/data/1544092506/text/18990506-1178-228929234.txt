 THE ENGLISH MAIL. B.M.S. Orizaba at Albany. The Orient Co.'s R.M.S. Orizaba, A. M'Watt, commander, arrived at Albany 2 a.m yester day, en route from London 10 Sydney, via ports, and subsequently resumed her voyage ea&tward. The Sydney portion of her English mails, under date London, April 7, may be looked for here on Wednesday next, and the steamer herself about two days later.
