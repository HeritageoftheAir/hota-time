 BERLIN TO DARWIN IN 3½ DAYS Englishman's Proposal LONDON. May 31. Plans are being discussed by the naval airman, Lieutenant Cathcart- Jones. for an attempt to make a record flight from Berlin to Darwin in 3½ days. He will use a German Heinkel mono plane, fitted with a withdrawable undercarriage. A piquant touch of mystery is the announcement that a young unnamed Englishwoman will act as reserve pilot.
