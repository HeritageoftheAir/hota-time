 AIR HAH SERVICES. NEW HIGH-SPEED 'PLANE. Darwin in Seven Days from London LONDON, Juno lo.— Britain's fastest mail-carrying 'plane— a twin-engined ma chine built by Boulton and Paul, Ltd., of Norwich, to the order of the Air Ministry — has been successfully demonstrated at Norwich. The machine is designed for service on Empire routes and is capable of flying 1,000 miles non-stop. It has a speed of 150 miles per hour at half throttle and of 200 at top speed. Mails may be sorted on the 'plane en route and dropped and picked up without landing. Storage is provided for 1,0001b. of mail matter. It ia understood that a test flight to Australia is contemplated. The machine could reach Cape Town from London in three days, and Darwin in less than seven days.
