 IN FIVE DAYS. LONDON TO DARWIN. Projected Flight.. LONDON, October 19. . The "Daily Herald" states that three ex-Royal Air Force officers are planning to make a commercial flight from London to Darwin in five days, arriving on Christmas pay. After a tour of Australia and New Zealand, they will cross to the United States. They intend to carry 50 samples of British goods, and will exhibit them at every stopping place.
