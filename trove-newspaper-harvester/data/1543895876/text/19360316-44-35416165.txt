 Two Days' Delay To London- Bound Air Mail DARWIN, March 15. The Qantas Empire Airways plane, carrying London-bound mails, arrived at Darwin at 8 a.m. today more than two days late. The delay occurred in Queensland, where dense low clouds and heavy rain made flying conditions &nbsp; impossible for two days. &nbsp; The plane left for Singapore at 10.45 &nbsp; a.m. &nbsp;
