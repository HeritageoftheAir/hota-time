 CAPT. BERTRAM'S RETURN FLIGHT. Darwin to London in 6J Days. Capt. Hans Bertram, accompanied by Mr. G. V. Allen, will leave Point Cooke tomorrow for Darwin on the first stage of his flight to London. He hopes to reach his destination in 6% days, as follows: - First Day. - Darwin to Sourabaya, via Ulma. Second Day.-Medan, via Batavia. Third Day.-Allahabad, via Alcyab. Fourth Day.-Basra, via Gwaklar, Fifth Day.-Athens, via Aleppo. Sixth Day.-Marseilles, via Rome. Supplies, of petrol and oil are being arranged for by the Shell Coy.
