 FOR LONDON. Orama Leaves Sydney To-day The Orient S.N. Co. advise that they will despatch the R.M.S. Orama from No. 8 Woolloomooloo wharf at 3 p.m. to-day for London, via ports. The steamer Otrantb is due at Syd ney early this morning from London, via ports, and will berth at No. 7 Woolloomooloo wharf.
