 AUSTRALIA STAR FROM LONDON Due Sydney To-day Bound from London, via ports, the Blue Star Line's motor vessel Aus tralia Star is due at Sydney to-day and berths at No. 2 Woolloomooloo wharf at S a.m. The vessel will be despatched at 5 p.m. to-day for Glad stone to load homewards, returning, to Sydney and sailing again on May 2 for London, via ports.
