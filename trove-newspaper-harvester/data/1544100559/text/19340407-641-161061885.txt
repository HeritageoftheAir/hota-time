 OTIRA AT SYDNEY. Sails To-day for London and Liverpool. Dalgety and Co. Ltd. advise that the White Star-Aberdeen steamer Otira arrived at Sydney yesterday morning from Tyne, via Newcastle. She was berthed at No. 22 Pyrmont on arrival, and was removed to No., 2, Dalgety's wharf, last evening to continue load ing. She will be despatched from Syd ney to-day for London and Liverpool.
