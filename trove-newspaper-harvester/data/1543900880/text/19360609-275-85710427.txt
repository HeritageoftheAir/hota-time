 Perth To Sydney In 24 Hours Less MELBOURNE, Tuesday. Twenty- four hours will he saved on the trip from Perth to Sydney when eight new railway engines, to be built in Queensland, are placed in service on the trans continental line within a year. The Commonwealth .Railways Commissioner (Mr. A. Gahan) said today that the train would leave Perth earlier and save about eight or nine hours from Kalgoor lie to Port Augusta. The construction of the new lino between Port Augusta and Ade laide would reduce the distance be tween these two points from 260 miles to 190. Passengers would reach Mel bourne from Adelaide about 18 hours earlier than at present, en abling them to connect with the Sydney express a day earlier.
