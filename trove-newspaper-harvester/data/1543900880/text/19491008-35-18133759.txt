 Perth-Sydney In Six Hours Planned - -- ? + . MELBOURNE, Friday. Two R.A.A.F. men hope to fly a Mosquito from Perth to Sydney in six hours to-morrow. An R.A.A.F. Lincoln bomber set a record of eight and a half hours for the 1,852 mile direct flight in September, 1947. Flight-Lieutenant C. Mel cerht will be pilot and Flight Lieutenant W. A. Davis will be navigator. They leave Guildford, Perth, at 8 a.m. (10 a.m. Eastern Standard Time). The Mosquito will return to its base with the No. 87 Sur- vey Squadron in Canberra.
