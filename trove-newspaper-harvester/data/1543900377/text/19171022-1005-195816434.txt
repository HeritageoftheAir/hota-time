 AIR SERVICES. LONDON TO MELBOURNE. TO NEW YORK IN TWENTY-FOUR HOURS. LONDON, Saturday, The "Daily Express" declares that, provided the weather is favorable, the Atlantic will be crossed by aeroplane be fore the end of the year. The route will be via the Azores, and New York will be . brought within 24 hours of London. The paper adds that plans are being made for regular air services to the Cape, India and Australia. Melbourne will be eight days and a half from London, India three days, and the Cape from four to live days. The speed will be 200 miles an hour, aud the cost to passengers will Jbe no more than the first-class fare by Sail ocean liner.
