 IN 40 HOURS London, Tuesday. - BOAC chairman (Sir Miles Thomas) today forecast a service of less than 40 hours from London to Sydney. "We hope by the end of 1951 or early in 1952 to operate the Comet on an express service to Karachi and Calcutta and later to Sydney," he said. The de Havilland Comet jet airliner yesterday set up a new London-Cairo record of 5 hours 7 minutes averaging 427 m.p.h. -"News" Special.
