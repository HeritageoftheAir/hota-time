 KINGSF0RD SMITH ATTEMPT ON RECORD LONDON TO MELBOURNE IN 71 HOURS ! SYDNEY, Thursday. Mr. John Stannage, secretary of the trans-Tasman Air Service Development Co., raid to-day that he expected Sir Charles Kingsford Smith would be able to leave London on Saturday on his flight to Australia, and may possibly arrive in Melbourne on October 15. This would bring him to Melbourne in under 71 hours, the record time set by Scott and Campbell-Black in winning the Centenary air race.
