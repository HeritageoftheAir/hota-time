 London-Sydney In 36 Hours LONDON, Wed. (A.A.P.)-. - The De Havilland Comet, Britain's first jet airliner, will fly' from London to Sydney in 36 hours when it makes its test flight to Australia and . New Zealand early next year, a De Havilland spokesman said today. "She won't be hurrying. She probably will land at Cairo, New Delhi, Singapore, Darwin, Sydney and Auckland," he said. "She will cruise about 500 m.p.h. and spend about 90 minutes on- the ground refuelling."
