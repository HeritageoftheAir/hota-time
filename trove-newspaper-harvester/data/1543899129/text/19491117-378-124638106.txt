 LONDON-SYDNEY FLIGHT IN 36 HOURS? LONDON, Nov. 6. - A De Havilland four Jet Comet will fly from London to Sydney in 36 hours when it makes its test flight to Australia and New Zealand early next year. A De Havilland spokesman said to-day, "She won't be hurrying. She will probably and at Cairo. New Delhi, Singapore, Darwin, Sydney. nd Auckland. She will cruise at about 500 m.p.h., and will Dpend about 90 minutes on the ground refuelling." The spokesman said that Comets will start on the regu lar Australian runs about 1953.
