LONDON, May 2.-"Flying wing" airliners cruising at 615 miles al hour at 40,000 feet and capable of carrying 72 passengers on Empire routes were forecast within ten years by Major Frank B. Halford, Chairman of the De Havilland Engine Co. Ltd., in an address to the Royal Society of Arts.
