 Flying Home LONDON TO SYDNEY IN 10 DAYS OAKLAND, July 16. Wing-Comniander Kingsford Smith has been approached by an Eastern company, which is constructing an ex perimental plane, regarding the pro posed Oakland to Tokyo flight, to take place before next June. His brother, Mr. R. H. K. Smith, said on Wednes day that the plane would be either a three or four-motored one, with a cruising radius of 6,000 miles. Wing-Commander Kingsford Smith is now en route for the East. He hopes to leave London on August 15 in a small English plane, on a projected ten-day six-stop flight to Sydney.
