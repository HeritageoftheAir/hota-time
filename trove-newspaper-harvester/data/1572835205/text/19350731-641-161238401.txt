 FROM LONDON. Oronsay Due Sydney To-day. The Or-ent Steam Navigation Co. advise that the steamer Oronsay is due at Sydney at 7.30 a.m. to-day from London, via ports, and will berth at No. 7 Woolloomooloo wharf. The ves sel will leave again on Thursday for a cruise to Rabaul, via Brisbane, and will return to Sydney on Aug. 14.
