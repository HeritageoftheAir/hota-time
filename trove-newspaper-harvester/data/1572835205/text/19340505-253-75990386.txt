 FERRIES FOR SALE May Steam Back to London SYDNEY. To-day. Three ferries of the Sara Kara type which were thrown idle with the opening of the Harbor Bridge have been offered for sale. If a London firm buys one, It will make the long voyage back to the land of its ?'birth.' The ferries are of a most seaworthy type, and came to Syd ney in easy stages via Sue and the northern eoast of Australia.
