 FLIGHT TO ENGLAND Monoplane Sets Out London in Five Days SYDNEY, Wednesday. Hundreds of friends, relatives and well wishers witnessed the monoplane Faith in Australia, take off from Rich- mond aerodrome to-day on a flight to Engladn. The machine left the ground after a run of 800 yards It carried more than 800 gallons of petrol Mr. C. P. T. Ulm said; "We are confident that we will be in England in a short while." It is understood that he has planned a route which will land him in London in five days. The crew comprises Messrs Ulm, P G. Taylor (navigator and co-pilot) and G. U. Allen (relief pilot and wire- less operator).
