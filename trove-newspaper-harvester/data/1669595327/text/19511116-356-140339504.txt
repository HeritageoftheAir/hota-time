 Sydney-London Three Days SYDNEY, Thursday. - Qantas Empire Airways and B.O.A.C., joint operators of the Kangaroo Air Service, had introduced a new and faster timetable between Syd. ney and London, a spokesman for Qantas said to-day. The service has been cut down to the elapsed time of three days. Elapsed time is time for the flight including stops. Previous elapsed time was four days. Under the new schedule, night stops at Karachi and Cairo have been eliminated on the west-bound flight, but on the cast-bound ser vices provision has been made for a night stop at Basra. Singapore will continue to be a night stop for services in each di. rection.
