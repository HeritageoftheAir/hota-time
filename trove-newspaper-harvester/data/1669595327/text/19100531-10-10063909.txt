 EMPIRE DAY. DAILY MAIL " CUP WON BY SYDNEY TEAM. LONDON, May 30 The scores in the shooting for the "Daily Mail" Cup have been completed, and as anticipated from the sensational score put up by the Grantville Technical College near Sydney, was won by that team, with the score of 810.
