 LONDON- BOUND. SOUTHERN CROSS LEAVES. Thirteen Days' Project. SYDNEY, Saturday. Squadron - Leader Kingsford Smith and Flight-Lieut C. T. P. Ulm left Richmond aerodrome at 10.46 o'clock this morning in the Southern Cross on the flight to Wyndham (1,860 miles). From there they will begin within a couple of days the long flight to Eng- land. With the aviators are Lieut. H. A. Litchfield (navigator), and Lieut T. W. McWilliam (wireless operator). The aviators aim to reach London in 13 days, although they claim that their object is not to break the record established by Squadron Leader Hinkler.
