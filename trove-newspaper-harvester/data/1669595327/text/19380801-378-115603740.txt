 London-Sydney in 5 Days. BRISBANE, July 31. London will be brought within five days of Sydney when the Royal Dutch Airline begins an additinnal service between Batavia and Amsterdam at the end of the year. The company now oper ates a four-day service three times weekly and will ineupurate a fourth. Night-flying will be part of the schedule. On the pre sent schedule it is expected a little over two days will elapse for the journey from Batavia to Sydney. The whole journey will occupy five days.
