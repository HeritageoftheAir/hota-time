 Renter's very Ltfest (Received in Sydney this day, at 1.35 p.m.) London, 2nd November. The English corn market has advanced Is 6d to 2s per quarter. Adelaide wheat is exceedingly scarce. The Royal Mail Steamer China arrived at Galle, from Australia, on the 31st October. Great confusion exists in France, owing to the conflicting views of the Monarchists and Republicans. The Left Centre demands the consolidation of the Republic, and the majority of the Press considers the prolongation of President M'Mahon's powers as the only solution of the present political position. The Tichborne case is adjourned. ,
