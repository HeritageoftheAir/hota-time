 THE MOLDAVIA. Due Sydney To-day from London. Macdonald, Hamilton & Co. advise that the P. & O. steamer Moldavia is due at Sydney early this morning from London, via ports, and will berth at No. 4 West Circular Quay. She will move round to No. 20 Pyr mont wharf later on in the day to discharge and will sail from there at 11.30 a.m. on Saturday for Brisbane.
