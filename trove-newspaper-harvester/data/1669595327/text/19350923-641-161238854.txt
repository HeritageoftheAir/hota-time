 THE KITTAWAKE. Due Sydney To-day from - London. Jas. Patrick & Co. advise that the steamer Kittiwake is due at Sydney at 7 a.m. to-day from London, and will ber.th at No. 36 Darling Harbour wharf. The vessel will remain in Sydney, for a few weeks for docking and sur veying. The Kittiwake will be renamed Car dross, and the present vessel of that name will be taken over by the John Burke Line.
