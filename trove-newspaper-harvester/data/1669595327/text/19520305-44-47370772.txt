 London Air Link Six Days A Week SYDNEY, March 4. From today, Sydney will have an almost daily air link with London. Qantas Empire Airways Ltd. and BOAC announced tonight that six services a week would now operate on the England-London Kan- &nbsp; garoo service, instead of five as previously. Services from Sydney to London via Calcutta will be operated by Qantas on Tues- days and Thursdays and by BOAC on Mondays, Wed- nesdays and Fridays. Services via Colombo will be operated by Qantas, de- parting Sydney on Satur- days. Services from London will be operated by Qantas, leav- ing on Tuesdays and Wed- nesdays, and by BOAC on Mondays, Thursdays and Saturdays.
