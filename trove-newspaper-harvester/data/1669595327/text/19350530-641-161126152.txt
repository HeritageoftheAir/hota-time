 CHITRAL FROM LONDON. Due Sydney To-day. Macdonald, . Hamilton & Co., Ltd., advise that the P. & O. steamer Chitral is due at Sydney at 7.30 a.m. to-day laden with general cargo from London, via ports, and on arrival will berth at No. 20, Pyrmont' wharf. The vessel will leave Sydney on Saturday for Brisbane to complete discharge and to commence loading outwards. ' .
