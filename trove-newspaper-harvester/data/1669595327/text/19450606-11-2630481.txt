 SYDNEY TO LONDON IN THREE DAYS LONDON, Tuesday. The Lancastrian from Sydney arrived in England at 7.58 G.M.T, to-day, the actual flying time for the trip being 51 hrs. 59 mins. The Lancastrian was 80 hrs. 28 mins, on the journey. It left Sydney at 11.31 G.M.T. on June 1 and made a night stop at Karachi.
