 IN TEN DAYS. SYDNEY—LONDON AIR MAIL. SYDNEY, Sunday. — Sir Charles Kingsford Smith, who wiil tender for the England—Australia air mail service, in the course of an interview said that, with a suitable machine there was no reason why a reliable air service should not be operated on a basis of 10 days between Sydney and London, allowing ample time for a nightly rest at all stations on the journey.
