 VICAR'S VISIT TO SYDNEY. A Wedding Day Promise. LONDON, Sept 16. The Rev. Lloyd Armstrong, Vicar of Holy Trinity Church. Yeovil (Somerset), with Mrs. Armstrong, who is an Australian, will shortly leave for Sydney to visit his wife's father, who Is seriously 111. Mrs. Armstrong has not seen him for six years. Mr. Armstrong met his wife while he was a minister In Australia. She promised on her wedding day that she would visit her father In three years, but was prevented from doing so owing to her child's Illness. The parishioners have now contributed suffi- cient money to enable Mr. Armstrong to ac- company his wife.
